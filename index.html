03 — Our Story

Built in the
Trenches of IT

Fifteen years ago, a group of frustrated IT professionals made a decision that would change everything. Tired of watching brilliant businesses get crushed under the weight of bloated contracts, legacy systems, and vendors who'd rather upsell than solve — they built something different. Something honest. Something intelligent.

2009

The Beginning

Three IT managers and two senior developers left their corporate jobs with one mission: make enterprise-grade IT accessible to businesses that couldn't afford enterprise-grade prices. NexaCore was founded in a small office with massive ambitions — and zero compromise on quality.

2013

The AI Pivot

We saw the future before it arrived. While others were still running manual scripts and reactive support tickets, our team began integrating early machine learning models into network monitoring. Our clients' uptime went from 94% to 99.7% — practically overnight.

2017

Scaling the Vision

100 clients later, we expanded to a full-service IT solutions provider. We launched CloudShield — our proprietary AI security platform — and grew our team to 45 specialists across development, infrastructure, and IT management. Affordable no longer meant compromise; it meant smart.

2024

The New Standard

Today, NexaCore stands as proof that intelligence and affordability aren't opposites. With a team that has collectively seen it all — from server room disasters to cloud migrations at scale — we've made it our life's work to rewrite the rules of what IT management can be.
